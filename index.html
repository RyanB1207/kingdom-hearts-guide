Learning Trinity Abilities

  • Blue Trinity

    Defeat the Guard Armour in Traverse Town, District 2.

  • Red Trinity

    Lock the Deep Jungle keyhole.

  • Green Trinity

    Lock the Agrabah keyhole.

  • Yellow Trinity

    Complete the Phil, Pegasus, and Hercules Cup at the Coliseum.

  • White Trinity

    Defeat Riku in the first Hollow Bastion encounter.
