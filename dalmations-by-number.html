Dalmations: By Number

  • 1, 2, 3

    Mystical House

    Use Glide to access the treasure chest in the water behind the Mystical House.

  • 4, 5, 6

    Alleyway

    Behind the item shop in the alleyway. It is behind the Red Trinity Mark where Pluto first discovered Sora.

  • 7, 8, 9

    Item Workshop

    Item Workshop must be opened via Green Trinity Mark in the Accessory Shop.

  • 10, 11, 12

    Secret Waterway

    Located in a passage just past the large area, behind a column.

  • 13, 14, 15

    Queen's Castle

    From the Tea Party Garden, go through the door of the house. Release the latch on the opposite wall. The chest is on top of the hedge you appear on.

  • 16, 17, 18

    Lotus Forest

    In the far part of the area from the entrance are three mushrooms. Jump on the middle one, then the left one. From here you can jump to a leafy platform and the chest.

  • 19, 20, 21

    Tea Party Garden

    Enter Lotus Forest and use Glide to access the area above the mushroom pond. Go through the exit to end up on top of the hedges in Tea Party Garden.

  • 22, 23, 24

    Gates

    Upon entering the world, you should see two large statues. The trinity mark is located at the feet of the statue to the right of the door to the lobby.

  • 25, 26, 27

    Hippo's Lagoon

    Jump across the water hole using trees and hippos. The chest is on an island at the end farthest from Camp.

  • 28, 29, 30

    Vines 2

    Cross the vines, then follow the platform at the end around to the chest. There will be a small gap you will need to jump.

  • 31, 32, 33

    Climbing Trees

    Accessible via Blue Trinity Mark located in front of the exit to the Tree House.

  • 34, 35, 36

    Camp

    The trinity mark is located near the large pile of crates and the work table.

  • 37, 38, 39

    Aladdin's House

    Aladdin's house is accessible by the poles in the Main Street area. It is marked as ??? before entering.

  • 40, 41, 42

    Bridge

    The chest is located on a platform to the left hand side of the bridge, if facing Oogie Boogie's place.

  • 43, 44, 45

    Clock Tower

    The chest is located on the lower level of the tower, adjacent the save point in one of the corners.

  • 46, 47, 48

    Palace Gates

    Jump on the stack of platforms in the corner, then Glide to the treasure chest on the center platform.

  • 49, 50, 51

    Entrance (Cave of Wonders)

    Above the rolling rock path in the entrance. You can use a barrel to jump up here, or use Glide.

  • 52, 53, 54

    Treasure Room

    Use "Call" command with Aladdin on the statue to the left of the Treasure Room. Sora will be blown by a gust of air and land next to the chest.

  • 55, 56, 57

    Chamber 3

    From Chamber 6, go to Chamber 3 via the lower area off the steps. The chest is near the entrance to this chamber.

  • 58, 59, 60

    Queen's Castle

    On one of the walls of the Bizarre Room, there is a tap (faucet). Hit this. The pot the water goes into leads to the chest (on the hedge next to you).

  • 61, 62, 63

    Grand Hall

    Off to the side of the keyhole area. Must defeat Riku before this chest appears.

  • 64, 65, 66

    Cemetery

    Use the second exit from Moonlight Hill. Only accessible after Halloween Town keyhole has been sealed, and Sora has left Halloween Town.

  • 67, 68, 69

    Guillotine Square

    Underneath the steps leading to the house with the pull chain.

  • 70, 71, 72

    Guillotine Square

    From the ledge opposite Guillotine Gate, Glide to the nearby stone head with two chests in the mouth.

  • 73, 74, 75

    Mouth

    After obtaining the High Jump ability, jump from the steps on the ship to the nearby platforms. At the top is the chest.

  • 76, 77, 78

    Chamber 3

    Enter from chamber 2. Destroy Barrel Spiders, but not the ordinary barrels. Use the barrels to get to the higher platform. The chest is up on the higher platform.

  • 79, 80, 81

    Chamber 5

    This chest is located on one of the higher platforms in the area. It is on top of a barrel at one end of the chamber.

  • 88, 89, 90

    Captain's Cabin

    The chest is located on the bed.

  • 82, 83, 84

    Ship's Hold

    After defeating Antisora, fly up to the rafters. There is a chest hidden on top of the central beam.

  • 85, 86, 87

    Ship's Hold

    Climb the ladder, and find the Trinity Mark.

  • 91, 92, 93

    Rising Falls

    Hidden behind the rocks immediately when starting the level.

  • 94, 95, 96

    Castle Gates

    From the upper platform, turn on the red crystal switch. Use the now-floating platform. Jump or Glide to the lower floating platform. Use Gravity to pull the floating chest down.

  • 97, 98, 99

    High Tower

    At the large block-moving area near the front castle emblem, cast Gravity on the floating treasure chest to bring it down.
